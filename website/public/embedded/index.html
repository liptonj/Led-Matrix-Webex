Connect to Your Display

Enter the IP address or hostname of your LED Matrix Display to get started.

Find this on your display's screen or in your router's device list.

How to find your display address:

  1. Power on your LED Matrix Display
  2. Connect it to your WiFi network
  3. The display will show its IP address during startup
  4. Or check your router for a device named "webex-display"
